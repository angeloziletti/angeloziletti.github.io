About Me

A machine learning & data scientist always on the quest for impactftul knowledge

I love applying and developing state-of-the-art natural language processing, deep learning, and unsupervised learning to solve complex problems in healthcare.

I have also have excellent communication skills with presentations to senior leadership, invited lectures, and talks at conferences worldwide.

My favorite techniques are:

  • Deep learning
  • Natural language processing (e.g., word embeddings, transformers, natural language generation)
  • Unsupervised learning (e.g., hierarchical clustering, non-linear dimensionality reduction)
  • Deep Learning applied to physics (e.g., crystal structure recognition)
  • Uncertainty estimation in deep learning (e.g., Bayesian deep learning and information theory)
  • Zero-shot learning
  • Intepretable machine learning
